How Online Banking works!


As you probably know, going to the bank is a huge pain! Online banking removes a lot of the hassles involved with going to the bank. You can view account balances, view recent transactions, download bank statements, pay third parties, take out loans, among other things. So how does it work? Read on or watch the video above to find out.

The first thing that happens is the user signs up, generally with their e-mail, a password, bank account info, and a short code from the bank to verify that it's actually you. This information is stored in the bank's datacenters.
